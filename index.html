Our Mission

We are commited to supporting women, children, orphans, and flood victims in Niger State and beyond. Through relief, empowerment, and education programs, we strive to create lasting changes in our communities.

What We Do

Flood Relief

Delivering food, clothing and shelter to flood affected victims in Niger State

Youth Sensitization

Educating teenagers on personal hygiene, career goals, and positive life choices

Featured Project

Flood Relief in Gwada

In 2023, Home of Ni'imah Support Foundation provided food supplies and relief materials to flood affected victims in Gwada, Niger State, reaching over 50 families. This project gave hope and support to vulnerable households in crises.
